Spanish Acoustic Corpus

Under the direction of Dr. Connor Mayer, our team is working on training supervised classification models to preform discriminant analysis on different acoustic cues and measurements. With over 6,400 audio tokens, we're also conducting outlier detection and data scrubbing to achieve a high quality data set.
